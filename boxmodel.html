Rainy Season or monsoon is the season categorized by heavy showers and pleasant weather. India, being an agrarian economy, heavily depends on the Rainy Season to provide enough irrigation for crops to grow. Rains bring a sense of calmness and tranquility with them and replenish Mother Nature in manifold ways.
